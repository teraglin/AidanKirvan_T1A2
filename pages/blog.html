A NEW PORTAL FOR TRPGS

Our Bare Owlbears is a TRPG portal for keeping track of active roleplaying games.

Thus far I have implemented a section for:

  • Players and their corresponding characters
  • Maps with travel routes marked out
  • A journal section for keeping track of campaign progress

In the future I will be looking into adding:

  • A calendar system for player availability
  • A calendar system to keep track of campain time frames, seasons and in-game festivals
  • Back end implimentation so that players can keep track of their own resources and commincate with one another

You can check out the progress of Our Bare Owlbears in PROJECTS.
